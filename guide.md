# Nano Banana Protein Power Smoothie

## The Recipe
- 100g mixed berries (Costco)
- 100g protein yogurt
- 1 scoop Optimum Nutrition whey protein
- 1 tbsp mixed seeds (pumpkin, sunflower, hemp)
- Large piece of fresh ginger
- Blend everything together

## Nutritional Values (per serving)
- Calories: 380 kcal
- Protein: 42g
- Carbohydrates: 28g
- Healthy fats: 9g
- Fibre: 5g

## Why It Works

**Berries** — A 2019 study in Advances in Nutrition found berries reduce inflammation. One of your NHS 5-a-day.

**Protein Yogurt** — Supports muscle repair. British Nutrition Foundation recommends protein at every meal.

**Whey Protein** — A 2017 Journal of Sports Nutrition review confirmed whey supports lean muscle and recovery.

**Mixed Seeds** — Pumpkin: high magnesium for sleep. Hemp: all 9 essential amino acids. Sunflower: vitamin E for skin.

**Ginger** — Used in South Indian cooking for 3,000 years. A 2015 study confirmed ginger reduces inflammation and supports digestion.

## South Indian Wisdom
In Tamil Nadu, ginger has been given to children daily for gut health and immunity for generations. Science now confirms why it works.

## Safety Note
Check with your GP before changing your diet.
